Welcome to Volla

Volla is a place for social story writing.

On Volla, people work together to create amazing stories in bite-sized chunks.

These stories, which we (slightly pretentiously) call Vollumes, are different to regular stories in that anyone, at any time, can suggest a different direction for the Vollume to take.

So if you're reading a Vollume and reach the end of what's been written so far, you can continue the story yourself!

Similarly, if you think of an interesting, alternative storyline for the Vollume you are currently reading, just scroll to the bottom of the page and start typing!
